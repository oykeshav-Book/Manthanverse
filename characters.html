Characters of Manthanverse

Different voices, different perspectives, different ways of seeing reality. Each character represents a unique approach to truth, consciousness, meaning and human life.

Advait

The Non-Dualist

Sees the world through the lens of oneness and consciousness. He constantly explores ideas such as Aham Brahmasmi, the nature of the self, and whether all minds are expressions of a single consciousness.

Keshav

The Playful Sage

A practical thinker who mixes wisdom, devotion and humor. Deeply influenced by non-dual ideas, he often jokes about being God himself, yet remains grounded in everyday life and devoted to Lord Rama.

Nakul

The Ordinary Man

Never claims to know the answers and rarely takes philosophy too seriously. Yet his simple questions and sense of humor often reveal what everyone else has overlooked.

Aarav

The Child

Curious, innocent and unafraid to ask what others overlook. He approaches the biggest questions of life with wonder rather than certainty.

Kashi

The Observer

Watches quietly, notices deeply and understands people. Rather than rushing to conclusions, she pays attention to human behavior, emotions and hidden motivations.

Gopi

The Devotee

Sees the world through love, faith and devotion to Krishna. Where others search through logic, she often finds meaning through surrender, trust and devotion.
